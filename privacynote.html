Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. This page explains how we collect, use, and protect your data at Schoolmait.

A Message for Students

Your privacy is of utmost importance to us at Schoolmait.

You have been directed to this page because you may have been asked by your school to complete a questionnaire about your wellbeing. It is important for us at Schoolmait.com, to ensure that you have a clear understanding of how we use, store and share the information you provide through our wellbeing questionnaire.


  • We would like to assure you that although the questionnaire is not anonymous, we only collect the minimum amount of personal information. For instance, we do not require you to provide your full name but instead ask you to use an alias.
  • We only share information that contains your alias with school staff for the purpose of supporting your academic progress. Our employees may access the information you provide, but they are unable to identify you as an individual based on the data you provide.
  • We currently DO NOT use artificial intelligence (AI) to generate survey questions or analyze responses. If we make such use in the future, we will provide a clear notice to participants before they begin the survey and clearly label any AI-generated content to ensure full transparency.
  • We take strong measures to protect the confidentiality of your information and prevent unauthorised access.
  • We may use some of the data you provide for other purposes, such as research, which can benefit other students over time. However, we ensure that any data shared is made anonymous so that it cannot be traced back to you as an individual.

The document below provides more detailed information on the types of data we collect, our use and storage of that data and how we secure it.

What data do we collect?

Our Company collects the following data:

  • Contact information. When you provide us with your contact information, whether through the use of our services, creation of an account, a form on our website, or an interaction with our team, we collect your contact information. This information may include, for example, your name, job title and email address.
  • Usage information. We collect usage data about you whenever you interact with our Services. This may include which web pages you visit, what you click on when you performed those actions, and other activities.
  • Device and browser data. We collect data from the device you use to access our Services, such as your IP address, operating system, browser details, and time of visit. This information may also tell us your location.
  • Log data. We keep log files that record data each time a device accesses our servers. The log files contain data about the nature of each access, including the originating IP address. We do this to maintain an audit trail of activity, to improve our Services, or to monitor or improve functionality.
  • Referral data. If as a Visitor, you navigate to our websites from an external source (such as a link on another website or via an email), we record information about the source that referred you to us.
  • Other data you submit. We may collect your personal information or data if you submit it to us in other contexts. For example, by giving us a testimonial, attending an event we host, opting in for free downloads, or purchasing our products or services. If you contact us we will also collect any information that you provide to us voluntarily in order for us to respond to your request.
  • Interacting with us on social media. We may collect personal information about you when you interact with us using social media. For example, if you post material to our LinkedIn page or tweet us on Twitter.
  • School information. If your are representing a school, we may collect from you information about your School, such as: School name and location, contact information of school team, and other account registration related details.
  • Questionnaire settings. If your are representing a school, we may collect from you information related to each survey conducted such as survey dates, and other questionnaire preference details.
  • Sensitive information. If you are a Participant, we collect a student alias as provided by the school. We do not collect information about your full name. Your school may provide us with personal details, such as demographic or academic information, for research purposes. We are committed to anonymizing this data to ensure your individual identity remains protected and cannot be traced.
  • Questionnaire data. If you are a Participant, we will collect and store your questionnaire responses unless you ask us to remove them by contacting us through this form.
  • Billing details. If you use a credit card for billing, our credit card processor may collect information such as the cardholder’s name, billing address, email address, credit card number, expiry date, and credit card security code.

Our Company collects your data so that we can:

  • If you are representing a school or an Administrator, we may use your data to provide you with our services, such us setting up and managing your account, and processing your orders. We may also use your data to contact you about the services or your account.
  • With your consent, we may use your data also to email you with special offers on other products and services we think you might like. You have the right at any time to ask us to stop contacting you for marketing purposes.
  • If you are a Student, we use your data to provide your School with questionnaire answers, analysis and results.

  • We may use artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning technologies to assist in summarizing insights, identifying trends, and generating reports for schools, or other activities. Any text, summary, or report generated using AI will be clearly marked as AI-generated. Schools and administrators will be informed when AI has played a role in producing insights.

  • When we process a Customer’s order, we may send your data to, and also use the resulting information from, credit reference agencies to prevent fraudulent purchases.
  • With your consent, we may display Customer or Participant testimonials on our websites. If you wish to update or delete your testimonial or any other endorsement, please contact us.
  • As we continue to develop our business, we may use data you provided if we are engaged in a negotiation to merge, sell or finance our Company.
  • In certain circumstances we may be required to share your data in order to comply with legal obligations.

How do we store your data?

Our Company securely stores your data at Amazon Web Services’ Frankfort data centre.

Our Company will keep questionnaire data including student alias and school name for up to 4 years. We may keep anonymised data to produce reports and share with third parties or for research purposes for indefinite time. No student or school will be identifiable in such reports.

We will retain other information for as long as is necessary to provide our services, or to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our legal rights.

What are your data protection rights?

Our Company would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:

  • The right to access - You have the right to request Our Company for copies of your personal data. We may charge you a small fee for this service.
  • The right to rectification - You have the right to request that Our Company correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request Our Company to complete information you believe is incomplete.
  • The right to erasure - You have the right to request that Our Company erase your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to restrict processing - You have the right to request that Our Company restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to object to processing - You have the right to object to Our Company's processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to data portability - You have the right to request Our Company to transfer the data that we have collected, in a structured, commonly used format, to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.

If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us.

What are cookies?

Cookies are text files placed on your computer to collect standard Internet log information and visitor behavior information. When you visit our websites, we may collect information from you automatically through cookies or similar technology.

For further information, visit www.allaboutcookies.org.
