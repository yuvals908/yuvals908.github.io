Student Wellbeing Assessment

A Comprehensive Student Wellbeing Assessment Solution

Product Goals

  • Evaluate students' wellbeing through online surveys to students, teachers and parents.
  • Get a holistic view of social, emotional, physical and digital wellbeing levels in school.
  • Identify strengths and areas for improvement, get evidence-based interventions ideas.

Ideal for

Primary (age 8+) and Secondary schools

Required Resources

  • Before the survey - 2-3 hours from School Admin / Wellbeing Lead to setup the survey and communicate to participants.
  • During the survey - the wellbeing survey takes approximately 10 minutes for students, 30 min for teachers and 5 minutes for parents to complete. Participants' access to connected devices is required (smartphones / tablets / computers).
  • After the survey - you get a detailed wellbeing reports. Additional staff time is needed to review the reports and plan interventions.

Privacy

We collect student aliases, instead of real names, so that school staff can recognize and support every student, while keeping student identities safer.
