Student Wellbeing Assessment

A Comprehensive Student Wellbeing Assessment Solution

Product Goals

  • Evaluate students' wellbeing through a set of online surveys to students, teachers and parents.
  • Provide a holistic view of social, emotional, physical and digital wellbeing levels in school.
  • Identify areas of strength and areas for improvement, and signpost possible evidence-based interventions.

Ideal for

Primary schools and Secondary schools (starting age 8+)

Required Resources

  • Before the survey - 2-3 hours from School Admin / Wellbeing Lead to setup the survey and communicate to participants.
  • During the survey - the survey takes approximately 10 minutes for students, 30 min for teachers and 5 minutes for parents to complete. Participants' access to connected devices is required (smartphones / tablets / laptops / computers).
  • After the survey - additional staff time is needed to review the reports, prepare and execute intervention plans.

Privacy

We collect student aliases, instead of real names, so that school staff can recognize and support every student, while keeping student identities safer.
