Teacher Wellbeing Check

This quick, free self-assessment includes 15 multiple-choice questions to help teachers reflect on their wellbeing at work. There are no right or wrong answers, just respond based on how you feel now. Once completed, you'll receive a personalized report with insights into your current state of wellbeing.

Note: This assessment is for informational purposes only. Schoolmait accepts no liability for any decision made based on this survey. The survey is not intended to address the specific circumstances of any particular individual and cannot replace professional advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Participation is completely voluntary.
Schoolmait does not collect or store the answers submitted through this survey.
