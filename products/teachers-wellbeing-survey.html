Teacher Wellbeing Survey

A Comprehensive Teacher Wellbeing Assessment Solution

Product Goals

  • Evaluate teachers' wellbeing through an online survey.
  • Provide a comprehensive view of teachers' wellbeing levels and identify areas of strength and areas for improvement.
  • Support schools in aligning wellbeing strategies, improve performance and retention rates.

Ideal for

Primary schools and Secondary schools

Required Resources

  • Before the survey - 1 hour from Survey Lead for setting up and communicating to participants.
  • During the survey - the survey takes up to 10 minutes to complete. Participants' access to connected devices is required.
  • After the survey - additional time is needed to review the reports, prepare and execute intervention plans.

Privacy

The teachers' survey is conducted anonymously to ensure privacy and encourage honest responses.
