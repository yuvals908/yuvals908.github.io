Student Wellbeing Challenges

Without a clear, school-wide picture of student wellbeing, it's hard to identify concerns before they affect learning and achievement.

Rising Mental Health Issues

An increasing number of students are dealing with anxiety, social isolation, and other wellbeing concerns that often go unnoticed yet if unaddressed can impact their academic journey.

Lack of Early Detection

Schools often lack a systematic way to identify students at risk, making it difficult to provide timely support before issues escalate.

Limited Data and Guidance

Without comprehensive data, it's challenging for schools to understand the full scope of student wellbeing and provide the interventions that help they really need.
