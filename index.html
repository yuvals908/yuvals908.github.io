Student Wellbeing Challenges

Today's educators recognize the vital role student wellbeing plays in academic success. However, many schools still operate without necessary tools to identify concerns before they affect learning and achievement.

Rising Mental Health Issues

Research shows that 1 in 7 students experiences mental health issues - yet most remain unrecognized and unsupported.

Lack of Early Detection

Without proactive measures, students who are struggling risk costly setbacks along their academic journey.

Limited Data and Guidance

Even well-meaning support efforts can miss rott causes and fail to provide students with the help they really need.
