Wellbeing Survey for School Advisors

Conducting a wellbeing survey as part of the advisory process can be a very powerful tool for educational consultants. With the results from the wellbeing survey, advisors can identify social groups and apply interventions and resources in an efficient manner.

Product Goals

  • To provide a holistic view of student wellbeing levels in school, each class and every student, and identify areas of strength and areas for improvement.
  • To signpost possible evidence-based interventions and promote better wellbeing among students.

Product Overview

This product allows educational advisors to evaluate students' wellbeing through a set of online surveys. A multi-perspective approach involves students, teachers and parents, to provide a holistic view of the wellbeing within school. The comprehensive analysis measures social, emotional and physical wellbeing of every student and signposts possible evidence-based interventions inspired by recent research in order to drive positive change.

Ideal for

  • Primary schools (Ages 8+)
  • Secondary schools

Privacy

We collect student aliases, instead of real names, so that school staff can recognize and support every student, while keeping student identities safer.
